# WEBSITE MAINTENANCE AND SERVICE-LEVEL SCHEDULE

## Plans
- **Handoff (€0/month):** final delivery and agreed launch support; later work quoted separately.
- **Technical care (from €59/month):** routine availability checks, dependency/security updates and small technical corrections.
- **Managed website (from €129/month):** technical care plus an agreed monthly allowance for ordinary text/image changes.
- **Managed growth (from €249/month):** managed care plus recurring content/search/conversion improvement work.

Prices are planning starting points. State VAT treatment, included monthly minutes/hours and third-party charges in the signed proposal.

## Priority definitions
- **Urgent:** production website unavailable or core enquiry path unusable.
- **High:** major visible fault with a practical workaround.
- **Normal:** content change, small defect or planned improvement.
- **Low:** future idea or non-urgent note.

## Response targets
Insert business-hours and response targets for each plan. A response target is not a guaranteed resolution time. No 24/7 support applies unless expressly purchased.

## Monitoring and maintenance
Define automatic/manual checks, maintenance windows, backup provider and retention, restoration testing, domain-renewal responsibility, SSL responsibility, and who pays platform usage.

## Exclusions
New features, redesigns, content production beyond allowance, third-party outages, account recovery outside Provider control, malware caused by Client credentials, and emergency work outside the plan are separately chargeable.
