# Halo: Earth Command module

Added an admin-only `/game` module to the Vercel/Neon dashboard.

## Maps

- Restored the original luminous-green Natural Earth world/coast/country boundary map as the strategic layer.
- Added drag, wheel zoom, double-click zoom, mobile controls and strategic location/unit overlays.
- Added a street-level OpenStreetMap surface layer with roads and city labels at all normal OSM zoom levels.
- Added real-place search through the dashboard's existing Google Geocoding endpoint.
- Added a draggable and zoomable orbital Earth layer with tethers, ODPs and fleets.
- Added a Sol-system framework with Earth active and later Moon/Mars/outer-system expansion slots.

## Strategy simulation

- Real-time/pause/time-speed controls.
- Credits, materials, energy, food, intelligence and manpower.
- Earth integrity and orbital-defense indicators.
- Ground, air, sea, orbital and space formations.
- Manufacturing queues and target-location deployment.
- Research projects with permanent effects.
- Diplomacy and faction relations.
- Custom unit-template builder.
- Fortification, repair and unit redeployment.
- Local browser autosave plus JSON export/import.
- Canon-event newspaper/bulletin system with outcomes modified by preparation.
- Combat pressure, local integrity, supply and territorial control.

## Lore handling

- Locations are marked `canon`, `canon-inferred`, or `speculative`.
- The module never labels New York's suggested future form or other extrapolations as official canon.
- Core 2552 events connected to the Battle for Earth are intentionally difficult or impossible to erase completely.
- No copyrighted Halo art, audio, models or extracted game assets are bundled.

## Important scope note

This is a playable Earth-first foundation, not a completed simulation of every Halo world and every unit in the franchise. The save and map architecture is designed so Luna, Mars, colonies, slipspace routes and later eras can be added without replacing the Earth module.
