10. Customer data ownership, export, deletion and post-termination retention.
11. Confidentiality, data processing and subprocessor use.
12. Security incident notification and cooperation.
13. Warranties, liability cap, indirect-loss exclusion and mandatory-law exceptions.
14. Suspension, cancellation, termination and refunds.
15. Changes to the service and terms.
16. Governing Finnish law and agreed dispute forum.
